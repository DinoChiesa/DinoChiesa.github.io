Notes
  1. This page uses the KJUR library (jsrsasign) for JWT decoding, verification, and generation, online, in the browser page.
  2. Information that you paste here, including Encoded JWT and keys, either private or public, never leaves your browser.
  3. The decoded header and payload shown here are pretty-printed.
  4. To verify a JWT: paste in the signed JWT, and your PEM-formatted public key, and click the verify button.
  5. To create a JWT: set the payload and the header, and paste in your PEM-formatted (PKCS#8) private key, and click the encode button.
  6. This page supports RSA keys only. When generating or verifying a token, the page supports signed tokens only, and only these algorithms: RS256, RS384, RS512, PS256, PS384, or PS512. For details on these algorithms, see the JWA specification.
