Notes
  1. This page supports RSA or ECDSA keys and algorithms (except for ES512, not supported by KJUR at this time). When generating or verifying a token, the page supports signed tokens only. For details on algorithms, see the JWA specification.
  2. To verify a JWT: paste in the signed JWT, and your PEM-formatted public key, and click the verify button.
  3. To create a JWT: set the payload and the header, and paste in your PEM-formatted (PKCS#8) private key, and click the encode button.
  4. On initial page load, the browser will generate a new RSA keypair and insert it into the appropriate boxes. You can replace either or both with your own values.
  5. You can generate a new keypair by clicking the appropriate button.
  6. Information that you paste here, including Encoded JWT and keys, either private or public, never leaves your browser.
  7. Ths page shows the decoded header and payload pretty-printed. The actual header and payload in a JWT do not have newlines.
